CASE 10

Which fraud pattern is quietly teaching every other pattern how to evolve?

Business insightCompares each pattern's yearly behavioural fingerprint (z-scored) to every other pattern's fingerprint one year later, flagging a match only above a similarity threshold, then counts how often each pattern repeats as the closest predecessor. Correlation-in-time, not proof of causation — a lead worth investigating, not a verdict.
